About the book

Marriage is the most important relationship any human being can have aside our relationship with our Maker. The truth is that almost everyone wants to be married and almost everyone fantasizes about a happy, blissful marriage. Unfortunately, not everyone gets those. In fact, the happy, exciting, wonderful, made-in-heaven marriage is a rarity. What is usual is marriages where the partners endure themselves.

About the Author

Anya C. Oko (PhD) is the founder and Housekeeper of Royalty Assembly International with churches in Lagos, Aba and Port-Harcourt. He has a mandate to raise Worldchangers, people who transform their world to the glory of God. He is an exceptional relationship coach, which made many young people refer to him as the ‘Love Doctor’. He is a pastor, leader, author and teacher. He has written other books including Love101, Let’s talk about Sex.
